What do these databases search?

Academic Search Premier is a multi-disciplinary database with full text for thousands of scholarly publications and peer-reviewed journals that is updated daily. It is a good general index to start one's research. JSTOR searches journal archives in the humanities, social sciences, and sciences. With a few exceptions, JSTOR titles do not include current issues. Science Directis a leading full-text scientific database offering journal articles and book chapters from more than 2,500 journals and almost 20,000 books. Web of Science indexes major journals across 150 scientific disciplines and includes all cited references captured from indexed articles.
